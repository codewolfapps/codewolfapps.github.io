Independent Android apps focused on privacy, reliability, and simplicity.

About

CodeWolf Apps is an independent Android development project. The apps are designed to work offline where possible, store data locally, and avoid unnecessary complexity.

The goal is straightforward: build tools that are reliable, clear, and easy to use.

Principles

  • Offline-first architecture
  • User privacy by default
  • Clean and minimal design
  • Long-term reliability
