Expense Tracker

Offline personal expense tracking for Android.

Overview

Expense Tracker is a simple Android app for tracking daily expenses and income.

All data is stored locally on your device. The app does not connect to banks, payment systems, or financial institutions.

It is designed for personal budgeting and record keeping.

Features

  • Track daily expenses and income
  • Create and manage budgets
  • Monitor savings goals
  • View reports and category breakdowns
  • Add notes to remember important details
  • Export backups with optional password encryption

Privacy & Data Handling

All financial data is stored locally on your device.

The app does not transmit your personal expense data to any server.

Encrypted backups can be exported manually by the user.

The developer does not have access to user data.

Advertising

This app displays ads through Google AdMob.

AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google’s advertising policies.

No financial data entered in the app is used for advertising purposes.

Disclaimer

Expense Tracker does not process payments, perform transactions, or provide financial, tax, or legal advice.

All data entered into the app is manually provided by the user and is for personal tracking purposes only.
