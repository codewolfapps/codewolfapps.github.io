MediaForge

Powerful image and video compression for Android.

Overview

MediaForge is an Android app designed to reduce the size of your images and videos while maintaining excellent visual quality.

Whether you're saving storage space, sharing files faster, or optimizing media for messaging and social platforms, MediaForge provides fast and efficient compression with a simple, modern interface.

Video compression is powered by FFmpeg, with optional desktop acceleration for supported computers on the same local network.

Features

  • Compress images with adjustable quality settings
  • Compress videos using FFmpeg
  • Batch compression for multiple files
  • Preview original and compressed file sizes
  • Optional desktop acceleration over local Wi-Fi
  • Fast local processing with no account required

Privacy & Data Handling

MediaForge processes your media locally on your device.

When desktop acceleration is enabled, files are transferred only between your Android device and your own computer on the same local network.

Your images, videos, and compressed files are never uploaded to CodeWolf servers.

The developer does not have access to your media files.
